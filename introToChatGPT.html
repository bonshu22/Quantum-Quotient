Introduction to ChatGPT

Course Overview

Explore the fascinating world of ChatGPT in this introductory course. Understand the fundamentals, capabilities, and applications of one of the most advanced language models.

Course Objectives

  • Gain a solid understanding of the architecture and technology behind ChatGPT.
  • Learn how to initiate conversations and interact effectively with ChatGPT.
  • Explore real-world applications and use cases for ChatGPT in various industries.
  • Understand the ethical considerations and best practices when using language models like ChatGPT.

Course Syllabus

Module 1: Introduction to ChatGPT

Overview of ChatGPT, its development, and key features.

Module 2: Getting Started

Practical guide on how to initiate conversations and make the most out of ChatGPT.

Module 3: Applications in Industry

Explore how ChatGPT is used in various industries, including healthcare, finance, and technology.

Module 4: Ethical Considerations

Discuss the ethical implications of using language models like ChatGPT and best practices for responsible AI.
