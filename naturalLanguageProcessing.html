Natural Language Processing with ChatGPT

Course Overview

Delve into the field of Natural Language Processing (NLP) with ChatGPT. This course covers the principles of NLP and how to leverage ChatGPT for language understanding and processing.

Course Objectives

  • Understand the basics of Natural Language Processing and its applications.
  • Explore the capabilities of ChatGPT in language understanding and generation.
  • Learn how to implement NLP techniques using ChatGPT in various domains.
  • Gain insights into the challenges and ethical considerations in NLP applications.

Course Syllabus

Module 1: Introduction to Natural Language Processing

Explore the fundamentals of NLP, its applications, and the role of ChatGPT in language processing.

Module 2: ChatGPT for Language Understanding

Learn how ChatGPT can be used to understand and interpret natural language input.

Module 3: NLP Techniques with ChatGPT

Implement various NLP techniques using ChatGPT, including sentiment analysis, named entity recognition, and more.

Module 4: Challenges and Ethics in NLP

Discuss the challenges and ethical considerations in implementing NLP applications, and best practices for responsible use.
